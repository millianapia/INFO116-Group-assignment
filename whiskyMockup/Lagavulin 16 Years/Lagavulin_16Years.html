Lagavulin 16 Years   About the distillery

The Lagavulin 16 is an Islay classic with a high peat content. The stills of the distillery are pear-shaped, so all the flavours are kept in the malt. The Lagavulin 16 single malt is matured in bourbon and sherry casks.
